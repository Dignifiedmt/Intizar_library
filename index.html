About This Library

Preserving and disseminating Mahdawiyyah knowledge

Our Mission

This digital library is maintained by Intizarul Imamul Muntazar, dedicated to preserving and disseminating authentic resources on the Mahdawiyyah doctrine.

Our mission is to provide free access to scholarly works, publications, and educational materials related to the awaited Imam Mahdi (AJF) and the contemporary Mahdawiyyah movement.

Collection Scope

The library features:

  • Classical texts on Imam Mahdi (AJF)
  • Contemporary analyses and research
  • Publications by Intizarul Imamul Muntazar
  • Educational resources for students
  • Multimedia materials and lectures
